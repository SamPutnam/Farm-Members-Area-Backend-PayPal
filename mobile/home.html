We provide an NMP database for you to enter all of the information required for your farm under Code 590. All you do is fill in your information once, download the automatically generated PDF, print it out, and send it to your state agricultural office.

Farmers know more about their crops than anyone else. No amount of data and online tools can tell them more about their herd or crop than they already know.


Code 590 Farm is simple. We don't tell you how to manage their farm. We just help you organize what you already know and keep your farm legal. We have blank forms for every piece of information about your farm that the state requires and we compile it into a PDF that you can download and keep on file.
