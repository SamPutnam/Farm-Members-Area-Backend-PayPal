FAQ


Why is Code 590 Farm less expensive than other Code 590 subscription-based services?

It doesn't cost us much to run this thing. We really just need to cover server hosting.  


Besides, we're farmers. We don't need much. And we don't want to check our bank account statement every month for subscription charges. We started Code 590 Farm because we wanted our farm management to be simple. We like to keep our books in good standing and follow the law with annual NMP requirements, but we don't need much else.



Who runs Code 590 Farm?

We are a small group of Vermont farmers who have been living in the state for 3 generations. We're based in Central Vermont, but we work with farmers and small companies around the state to keep our equipment up to date and our crops and herd healthy.


We started Code 590 Farm in 2016 to help other farmers comply with the new Code 590 law out of Colorado that was just adopted in Vermont and is on its way to adoption in other states. We started with friends and family and expanded to offering our service to everyone for a low, one-time fee.



We use industry standard security practices. We do not have access to the information about your farm that you enter. Code 590 is not affiliated with the government or the National Agriculture Association.
